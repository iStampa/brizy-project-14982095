who is the ops group

We are a full-service general contracting and construction management firm specializing in all phases of construction.  With a primary focus on Roofing, Plumbing, Brick/Stucco and flooring. Our firm recognizes all phases of construction including design, project execution and customer service.


We believe in giving back, so we volunteer our time and support organizations that directly impact our veterans and first responders and have donated for years to those organizations. .

Carlos Rodri

FOUNDER

Carlos served our country as an Army Medic for six years and now serves in the U.S. Air Force Reserve in a Logistic Readiness Squadron. He attended Durham University in Northern England for his executive MBA and obtained a finance degree from the University of Las Vegas, Nevada. 

 

His extensive investment experience, combined with his background in running family offices and commercial real estate, makes him a valuable partner to The Ops Groups team. His previous employers include UBS, Merrill Lynch, PaineWebber, and Bank of America Private Bank.

 

Service before self is important to him so he volunteers as an Election Judge with Dallas County Elections and served with the Texas State Guard during natural disasters and state emergencies until enlisting in the U.S. Air Force.
